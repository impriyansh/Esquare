About Us

Can engineers be entrepreneurs? Or is their fate sealed in the B.Tech degree they acquire? A group of engineering students asked themselves this very question and realized that the Indian education system was not able to provide them with the practical knowledge or the confidence required for facing the challenges of the working environment. At the end of the day, an average day at work is more than trigonometry and free body diagrams. Imagine being thrown into an ocean without knowing how to swim! So, an idea of an organization was formulated, one that would provide an opportunity to students to get access to industries before being placed. The industries, hence, would be able to screen new recruits in a more effective manner.
